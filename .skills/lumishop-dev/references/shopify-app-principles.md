# Shopify App Principles

## Product principles
- Merchant setup should feel low-friction.
- Product video generation should feel one-click where possible.
- Storefront impact must remain light.
- Value proposition should be conversion-oriented, not AI-demo-oriented.

## Technical principles
- Verify webhook signatures.
- Keep auth/session handling isolated.
- Prefer lazy video loading and poster-first delivery.
- Favor deterministic templates for fast iteration.
- Design for future analytics and A/B testing.
